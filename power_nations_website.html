🌍 World Power Nations
USA China Russia Germany France UK India Japan Turkey Brazil South Korea Saudi Arabia Israel Iran Egypt

🇺🇸 USA

Politics: Federal republic, President Joe Biden.

Military: World's largest military budget, nuclear arsenal.

Geography: North America, vast and diverse climate.

History: Independence in 1776, global superpower since WWII.

🇨🇳 China

Politics: Communist one-party state, Xi Jinping.

Military: Largest standing army, nuclear power.

Geography: East Asia, diverse landscapes.

History: Ancient civilization, People's Republic founded in 1949.

🇷🇺 Russia

Politics: Federal semi-presidential republic, led by Vladimir Putin.

Military: Major nuclear power, large military forces.

Geography: Largest country, spans Europe and Asia.

History: Russian Empire, Soviet Union, modern state since 1991.

🇩🇪 Germany

Politics: Federal parliamentary republic, Chancellor Olaf Scholz.

Military: NATO member, modernizing armed forces.

Geography: Central Europe, bordered by 9 countries.

History: Unified in 1871, WWII, reunification in 1990.

🇫🇷 France

Politics: Semi-presidential republic, President Emmanuel Macron.

Military: Nuclear power, active globally.

Geography: Western Europe, Mediterranean and Atlantic coasts.

History: French Revolution, colonial empire, EU founding member.

🇬🇧 United Kingdom

Politics: Constitutional monarchy, Prime Minister Rishi Sunak.

Military: Nuclear power, global influence.

Geography: Island nation, consists of 4 countries.

History: British Empire, WWII, Brexit in 2020.

🇮🇳 India

Politics: Federal parliamentary republic, Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Military: Large army, nuclear power.

Geography: South Asia, diverse climate and terrain.

History: Ancient civilization, independence from Britain in 1947.

🇯🇵 Japan

Politics: Constitutional monarchy with parliamentary democracy.

Military: Self-Defense Forces, technologically advanced.

Geography: Island nation in East Asia.

History: Samurai era, WWII, postwar economic miracle.

🇹🇷 Turkey

Politics: Presidential republic, led by Recep Tayyip Erdoğan.

Military: NATO member, large and active forces.

Geography: Bridges Europe and Asia.

History: Ottoman Empire, republic founded in 1923.

🇧🇷 Brazil

Politics: Federal presidential republic.

Military: Largest in Latin America.

Geography: Covers much of South America.

History: Portuguese colony, became independent in 1822.

🇰🇷 South Korea

Politics: Presidential republic.

Military: Highly trained and modernized.

Geography: Korean peninsula, mountainous terrain.

History: Rapid development since Korean War.

🇸🇦 Saudi Arabia

Politics: Absolute monarchy.

Military: Well-funded, large arsenal.

Geography: Desert nation in the Middle East.

History: Founded in 1932, major oil producer.

🇮🇱 Israel

Politics: Parliamentary democracy.

Military: Mandatory service, nuclear ambiguity.

Geography: Eastern Mediterranean region.

History: Founded in 1948, involved in regional conflicts.

🇮🇷 Iran

Politics: Islamic republic with supreme leader.

Military: Large regional power.

Geography: Mountainous, central Middle East.

History: Ancient Persia, Islamic Revolution in 1979.

🇪🇬 Egypt

Politics: Semi-presidential republic.

Military: Largest in Africa.

Geography: Northeast Africa, Nile River.

History: Ancient civilization, modern state since 1952 revolution.
